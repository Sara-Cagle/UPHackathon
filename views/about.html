Sleeping is really important. Here is a chart:


Score Risk Response/Actions
9 or more High Document externally, do not engage in any safety critical work and do not recommence until fit for work.
5 - 8 Moderate Report to supervisor and document. Organise supervisory checks. Complete symptom checklist and task re-assignment.
1 - 4 Low Report to the supervisor and document. Undertake approved individual control measures. Self-monitor for symptoms, team monitoring by colleagues and task rotation.
0 n/a Remain with working existing arrangements unless higher level hazards are present.
