This app should be used once daily to maintain an effective schedule and allow for easier analysis of job qualification.

The Fatigue Figure calculated in this app helps to determine the ability for the user to work their upcoming shifts. It is calculated by analyzing your previous 24 and 48 hours of sleep, including naps.

If you have been unable to maintain an effective sleep schedule, regardless of the reason, it is unsafe for you to work. If your Fatigue Figure is high, an alert will be sent to your supervisor, requesting a change in your work schedule. If your Fatigue Figure is low, you will be free to continue your work as normal.

This chart explains what each Fatigue Figure means in relation to your ability to work.


Score Risk Response/Actions
9 or more High Document externally, do not engage in any safety critical work and do not recommence until fit for work.
5 - 8 Moderate Report to supervisor and document. Organise supervisory checks. Complete symptom checklist and task re-assignment.
1 - 4 Low Report to the supervisor and document. Undertake approved individual control measures. Self-monitor for symptoms, team monitoring by colleagues and task rotation.
0 n/a Remain with working existing arrangements unless higher level hazards are present.
